Mask/Face Covering Resources

Surgical masks and N95 respirators are in short supply and should be reserved for healthcare workers or other medical first responders, as recommended by CDC guidance. The CDC recommends wearing cloth face coverings in public settings where other social distancing measures are difficult to maintain to slow the spread of the virus.

Clinical Informatics Research Group, University of Washington.

This page was last updated 7-Apr-2020, but the resources we point to are often updated daily or more frequently.
